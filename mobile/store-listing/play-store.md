## Full Description (4000 chars max)
OMJI is Balingasag's all-in-one transport, delivery, and food ordering platform. Whether you need a ride across town, want to send a package, or crave food from your favorite local restaurant — OMJI has you covered.

**PASUNDO - Ride Service**
Book safe, affordable motorcycle and tricycle rides instantly. See real-time driver location, estimated fare, and arrival time before you book. Track your ride live on the map.

**PASUGO - Delivery Service**
Send documents, packages, and parcels across Balingasag with real-time tracking. Take a photo of your item, choose the package size, and let our riders handle the rest.

**ORDER FROM LOCAL STORES**
Browse menus from restaurants, grocery stores, and shops in Balingasag. Add items to your cart, pay with cash, GCash, or Maya, and get your order delivered to your doorstep.

**PASABAY - Ride Sharing**
Share rides with other passengers heading the same direction. Save money and reduce traffic — it's a win-win!

**Key Features:**
- Real-time GPS tracking for all rides and deliveries
- Multiple payment options: Cash, GCash, Maya, Wallet
- In-app chat with your rider
- Rate and review your experience
- Save favorite stores and addresses
- Refer friends and earn rewards
- Push notifications for ride updates
- Available in English and Cebuano/Bisaya

**Why OMJI?**
Built by locals, for locals. OMJI understands the unique needs of Balingasag. Our riders are verified, our fares are transparent, and our service is reliable. Join thousands of Balingasaganons who trust OMJI for their daily transport and delivery needs.

Download OMJI today and experience the future of local transport!

## Description
OMJI is Balingasag's all-in-one platform for rides, deliveries, and food orders.

PASUNDO - RIDE SERVICE
Book motorcycle and tricycle rides instantly. Track your driver in real-time on an interactive map. See your fare estimate before you book.

PASUGO - DELIVERY SERVICE
Send packages and documents across town. Photo your item, pick a size, and track delivery from pickup to drop-off.

LOCAL STORE ORDERS
Browse menus from Balingasag's restaurants and shops. Order food, groceries, and more — delivered to your door.

PASABAY - RIDE SHARING
Share rides with passengers going your way. Save money while helping reduce traffic.

FEATURES:
• Real-time GPS tracking
• Cash, GCash, Maya & wallet payments
• In-app chat with riders
• Rate & review system
• Favorite stores & saved addresses
• Referral rewards program
• English & Cebuano support
• Dark mode

Built by locals, for locals. OMJI is designed for Balingasag's unique transport needs with verified riders and transparent pricing.
